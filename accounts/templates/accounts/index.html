Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions to find quick answers to common inquiries. Whether you're curious about our services, support, or processes, this section is here to help you get the information you need—fast and hassle-free.

How do I open an account with Starlink Bank online?

You can open an account online by visiting our website and selecting the type of account you'd like to open (savings, checking, etc.). You’ll need to provide basic information, such as your name, address, and social security number. You’ll also need to submit identification documents like a driver’s license or passport. You can complete the process in a few simple steps and start banking right away!.

How secure is Starlink Bank’s online banking platform?

Our online banking platform is built with the latest security features, including encryption and two-factor authentication (2FA). We also offer biometric login options for mobile banking. Your data is always protected by industry-leading measures, and our system is regularly updated to safeguard against any emerging security threats.

Can I apply for a loan or credit card online with Starlink Bank?

Yes, you can apply for loans and credit cards directly through our online platform. Simply visit our "Loans" or "Credit Cards" section, fill out the application form, and submit any required documents. We will review your application and get back to you with a decision in a timely manner.

How can I transfer money between my accounts or to another bank online?

Transferring money is easy through our online banking platform. You can transfer funds between your own accounts with a few clicks. To send money to another bank, use our External Transfers feature. All transfers are secure and typically processed within 1–3 business days, depending on the transaction.
